💡 Success Tips

For Major Projects

Start early and embrace the process - your best work comes from iteration
Take creative risks - this is your time to experiment without fear
Document everything: progress photos, sketches, and reflections
Seek feedback during work time, not just at project completion

For Skill Building

Practice techniques outside of class when possible
Study the artist exemplars provided with each unit
Connect assignments to your personal interests and experiences
Maintain a consistent sketchbook or digital idea journal

For Professional Growth

Care for supplies and maintain an organized workspace
Meet deadlines consistently and communicate early if issues arise
Participate actively in critiques - both giving and receiving feedback
Begin building your portfolio mindset from day one
